## App Store submission pack (draft)

### App name

### Subtitle
- Home Energy Audit, Simplified

### Promotional text
Run a guided home energy audit in minutes. Scan rooms, equipment, appliances, and bills to get a clear grade, prioritized upgrades, and an exportable report.

### Description
Manor OS helps you understand where your home is wasting energy—without spreadsheets.

**What you can do**
- Guided audit flow from rooms to equipment to bills
- Get an efficiency grade with confidence cues
- See where your energy goes (breakdowns + top consumers)
- Compare bill-based usage vs audit-based estimates
- Get prioritized upgrades with savings + payback estimates
- Export a shareable PDF report

**Designed for privacy**
- Works offline
- Stores audit data locally on your device
- Optional notifications
