Privacy Policy

Last updated: Feb 25, 2026

Summary

Manor OS is designed to work offline and store your audit data locally on your device. We do not sell your data.

Data we store

  • Homes, rooms, equipment, appliances, and bill details you enter
  • Photos you capture for equipment labels, appliances, lighting, and bills (if you choose to save them)
  • App preferences (e.g. onboarding completion, energy rates) stored in UserDefaults

Data we collect

By default, Manor OS does not collect personal data on a server. If analytics or crash reporting are introduced in the future, we will update this policy and the in-app disclosures before enabling it.

Permissions

  • Camera: used to capture photos for scanning equipment labels, appliances, lighting, and utility bills.
  • Photos: used only if you choose a bill image from your photo library.
  • Location: optional, used to infer climate zone to improve calculations.
  • Notifications: optional, used for reminders and tips if enabled.
