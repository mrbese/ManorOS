The Methodology

The Battery Synergy Thesis

An inefficient building envelope directly cannibalizes the value of home battery systems. A poorly insulated home draws 5-6 kW on a summer afternoon, leaving a battery inverter exporting barely half its rated output during peak grid events when electricity prices spike.

ManorOS quantifies this: for each home, it calculates how much additional battery export capacity passive efficiency upgrades would unlock. Translate passive efficiency into active grid revenue.

1.5–2 kW
Additional export capacity unlocked by upgrading attic insulation to R-49 and sealing ducts.
30–40%
More grid revenue with zero additional battery hardware cost.
